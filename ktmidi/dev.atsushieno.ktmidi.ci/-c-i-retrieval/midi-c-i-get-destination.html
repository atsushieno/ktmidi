midiCIGetDestination

fun midiCIGetDestination(sysex: List<Byte>): Byte

retrieves destination channel or "whole MIDI Port" from a MIDI-CI sysex7 chunk. The argument sysex bytestream is NOT specific to MIDI 1.0 bytestream and thus does NOT contain F0 and F7 (i.e. starts with 0xFE, xx, 0x0D...)
